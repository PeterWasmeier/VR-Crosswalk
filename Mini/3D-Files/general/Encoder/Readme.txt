This is a "Wisamic" Encoder, used by the "VR-Crosswalk Mini" (not being used by its big brother).
300 Pulses/Rotation/Channel. In total 1200 pulses/rotation with falling and raising pulses.

Wires:
	black: GND
	red: VCC (+5V)
	white: Channel A
	green: Channel B
